# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful, inclusive, and free from harassment.

## Expected behaviour

- Be constructive and professional.
- Discuss ideas and code rather than attacking people.
- Respect privacy, licences, and data-usage restrictions.
- Welcome questions from contributors with different experience levels.

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behaviour may be reported through the repository's private security or maintainer contact channels. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate this code.
