## 01. Portfolio positioning statement

**Objective:** Turn portfolio positioning statement into a concrete recruiter-facing asset or repeatable action.

### Core explanation
- Lead with the business problem and visible product outcome.
- Use metrics only with clear dataset and validation context.
- Provide one-click proof through the live demo and repository.
- State personal ownership and collaboration credit accurately.
- Make the next action obvious for a recruiter or hiring manager.

Every claim should be traceable to the live app, repository, model card, or merged QA evidence.
